Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 cup ghee
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup curd
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup water

Recipe

  1. Take a bowl and add milk powder, all-purpose flour, baking soda, ghee, milk, curd, and cardamom powder. Mix it well and make a soft dough.
  2. Make small balls from the dough and keep it aside.
  3. Heat ghee in a pan and fry the balls on low flame until it turns golden brown.
  4. Take another pan and add sugar and water. Boil it until the sugar dissolves.
  5. Now add the fried balls in the sugar syrup and let it soak for 2 hours.
  6. Now Gulab Jamun is ready to serve.

Coconut Barfi

Ingredients

  • 1 cup coconut
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup ghee
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • 1/4 cup cashew nuts
  • 1/4 cup almonds
  • 1/4 cup pistachios

Recipe

  1. Take a pan and add coconut, sugar, milk, and ghee. Mix it well and cook it on low flame.
  2. Add cardamom powder, cashew nuts, almonds, and pistachios.
  3. Mix it well and cook it until it thickens.
  4. Now pour the mixture into a greased plate and let it cool.
  5. Cut it into pieces and serve.

Rasmalai

Ingredients

  • 1 liter milk
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • 1/4 cup almonds
  • 1/4 cup pistachios
  • 1/4 cup saffron

Recipe

  1. Take a pan and add milk. Boil it and add vinegar. Stir it well until the milk curdles.
  2. Now strain the curdled milk and wash it with water.
  3. Squeeze the water and make a dough. Make small balls from the dough.
  4. Take a pan and add sugar and water. Boil it until the sugar dissolves.
  5. Now add the balls in the sugar syrup and cook it for 10 minutes.
  6. Add cardamom powder, almonds, pistachios, and saffron.
  7. Now Rasmalai is ready to serve.
